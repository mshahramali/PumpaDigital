Setup Instructions
Step 1 — Create Tally Form (Free)
Go to tally.so → Create form with fields: Name, Phone, Email, Date of Birth
Step 2 — Add Webhook
In Tally form settings → Integrations → Webhooks → Add your server URL
Step 3 — Generate QR Code
Copy your Tally form URL → Go to qr-code-generator.com → Generate & download
Step 4 — Print & Place
Print QR code → Place on restaurant tables, counter, menu cards
